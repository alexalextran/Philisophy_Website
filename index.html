Hello!, Before you start reading, i wanted to give a quick thank you for visiting my website, this was orignally a little journal but i decided to turn it into a Philsopy website that i can share with everyone :)

Also please take a look at the left side where you can find songs to play in the background while reading some of these!


P1 This world is hell, the only thing keeping people going are their hopes and dreams. Destroy that and you destroy their will to live. (I’ve seen it in action)

P2 The hardest pill to swallow is that no matter how much love and effort you put into a person, you can’t force them to stay. Does this mean love is a choice?

P3 Are the connections/memories we make with people worth the pain and suffering of losing them? -Love is just a few moments of pure happiness for a lifetime of sadness

P4 What is a monster? -People who understand what they are doing is wrong but still do it regardless even if it has no logical reason

P5 Is it better to live in blissful happiness or live with a painful truth? (people who live in bliss tend to live longer)

P6 People live for all sorts of things, to raise children, to discover all science has to offer, to see where they end up in the future. Does this mean that humans have no purpose and instead create their own?

P7 By trying to find happiness all you find is a list of reasons of why you’re unhappy

P8 Do we live to be remembered by others, making enough friends to have a full funeral?

P9 Is there more suffering and pain in this world than happiness and content? Or are we just hardwired to focus on those aspects of being human ?

P10 Does math really exist or is it something we create to help explain the rules of the universe. (What i mean is, what if aliens found a way to explain the universe without using math, without using the concept of numbers)

P11 Do we set children up for failure by creating an unrealistic prediction of life?

P12 I am never able to feel as if my feelings are valid, there is someone who has it worse. But does this mean I should ignore my own feelings?

P13 Has depression flipped evolution on its head? , instead of fighting to live, you no longer want to live

P14 Why are humans capable of feeling depression, what evolutionary advantage does this have?

P15 Each day someone is having the best day of their life, while someone is drowning in immense suffering

P16 If you tell someone life is meaningless would they spend their life living for themselves, enjoying whatever this world has to offer before they pass or would they stop living because life has no meaning

P17 How do we really define living? I feel as if i am existing, just taking care of myself because i have too

P18 Is there such a thing as free will if our future has already been determined? If all our decisions and choices will lead us to our future

P19 Do we all just distract ourselves from existentialism by keeping ourselves busy?

P20 We all secretly determine the worth of people's lives around us, placing the worth of loved ones highest

P21 I’ve realised my most favorite songs have an underlying sense of sadness, but aren't completely sad; shogeki, bones low roar; veil. Because that's how most of life has been not completely miserable but a underlying sense of sadness

P22 Why are humans so good at remembering the past yet so terrible at imagining the future

P23 We have so long to live, yet we only ‘live’ when we are close to death

P24 There exists a person on this earth at any given moment that has the right to say that he has it the worst in life

P25 Sometimes there are no villains, people do what they do because they must

P26 Do humans really have limitless potential? Or are we forever meant to stay within our own boundaries?

P27 What does a world without pain and suffering look like?

P28 Why do we assign colors to emotions? E.g “Im feeling kind of gray today”

P29 Why is it so easy for some people to help and understand others but struggle with themselves?

P30 The person we help grow and forgive the most should often be ourselves but is often not

P31 Can you teach a person to feel emotion? Someone who has never felt certain emotions?

P32 Arguably speaking, reincarnation is the worst possible punishment a dead person could go through.

P33 One of the only consistents about the human race is that we’re always changing in some way or another.

P34 Within our own lives there is no one that is ever consistent in our lives, people always come and go, even we change in different ways, humans are never consistent

P35 The deepest/strongest emotions are the ones that are often hardest to explain; depression, love, fear, anger, joy.

P36 The only universal language is violence

P37 Other species must find it strange that we attach emotional value to all sort of objects

P38 Why do we humans desperately want to find our purpose or reason for existence?

P39 Sometimes people don’t really get better, people just learn to live with it

P40 Is it a blessing to be able to forget? Are we privileged to be able to move on and forget painful memories or is it painful to not be able to remember the voices, faces and experiences of our loved ones?

P41 Sometimes playing sad music helps comfort people because it is comforting to know that others are similarly going through the same thing

P42 Living purely for someone else/others is dangerous

P43 Sometimes living for a potential is worth the venture but on the other hand someones a potential is not worth the venture

P44 It is easier to feel pain than feel absolutely nothing at all?

p45 The most beautiful people in this world are those who have known suffering, known loss and have found their way out of the depths. These people have an appreciation, a sensitivity and an understanding of life that fills them with compassion and a deep appreciation

Our capacity for evil gives meaning for every brave, compassionate choice that we make, becasue we made a choice to steer away from those atrocaties

Chose the version of yourself that you belive to be worth your time here on earth

P48 Perhaps it is better to sacrifice your life for a greater service, than to live it with the purpose of your own satisfaction, knowing nothing will ever really satisfy you or make you whole

P49 Alone in the womb and soon alone in the coffin — just as we are born in darkness we are buried in it; just as we taste the good, we are drowned in the lack thereof

P50 It is often not our enemies which hurt us the most but rather our own loved ones

P51 Healing is never a linear progress, reality sets in, dreams are broken but at the end of the day, it is necessary for growth.

P52 The worst part about loving someone is when they’re gone

P53 It's interesting how different people view death differently, for some it's a chance at a new life, an afterlife paradise, an escape from death or the inevitable end.

P54 In this day and age, we are the most connected yet we feel the loneliest

P55 Sometimes loneliness is not a result of the lack of physical intimacy but sometimes not being understood

P56 Is it possible to live a whole life without suffering or feeling any sort of negative emotions and vice versa. Does there exist a person who has never felt sadness or happiness?

P57 Growing up is just accepting life for what it is and not what it was described to you as

P58 Someone out there is doing the same thing as you in the same situation right now

P59 You don’t ever need a relationship to complete yourself, a relationship's purpose is to help you grow, and you are already capable of doing that alone

P60 No matter how hard, pain is necessary to grow, it enforces change.

P61 Learn to stop doing things that hurt you just to please others; whether it be a parent, partner, friend, stranger.

P62 What does it mean to find yourself? And how does one lose oneself?

P64 Isn't it ironic when we say we want someone to understand us but we put up emotional walls to protect ourselves?

P65 Some people fear expressing themselves because they have often been ridiculed for it in the past.

P66 If you could travel back in time to when you were a child what would you tell yourself?

P67 Loving someone is being content with their hapiness even if your aren't the reason

P68 They say that life and all other things that are ephemeral are beautiful because they decay, so much to say that even decay itself is beautiful.

P69 Do we limit ourselves based on our view of the world?

P70 It feels like such a grand endeavour to do so much as express yourself to other people now-a-days.

P71 Internet culture is terrifying. For something that could supply any kind of information at any time, some people only choose to believe some acute niche of it.

P72 If someone were to have no sense of morality, people who do have it would consider them as morally evil.

P73 Of all the times that any media attempts to depict a paradise, it’s often the case that there aren’t many people in it. Population density is arguably so uncomfortable to imagine or go through because of how many people you have to deal with.

P74 When shit happens, it’s best to not just think about it and let it eat away at you, but to just accept it and move on. Sounds easy but its not.

P75 The sun rises everyday. It sets every afternoon. The sun does this regardless of how you feel. A new day starts regardless of what you do. Don’t waste away your youth. It’s your responsibility to wake up and say to yourself “I’m going to do something today. Whether it be study, work, your mum or whatever. I’m going to do something”

P76 Hitting rock bottom really is a key point in your life. Will you take what you learned and motivate yourself up? Or will you acknowledge the state that you’re in and give up anyway. Kinda defines you and where your life will go.

P80 Life isn't about finding yourself, it's about creating yourself

P81 Its crazy how the same words can mean so much depending on the person

P82 The difference between war and hell is that hell takes the wicked

P83 You don’t ever need anyone to ‘complete yourself’ you are who you make yourself to be

P84 Life often shows us before it teaches us; we experience before we learn..

P85 Are we our biggest enemy, do we make our own problems

P87 Don't waste time on someone who makes you feel unsure or unsecure, holding all just tears you apart. No matter how strong the connection is

P87 what has this world come to. We wake up, check our phones, eat, check our phones, eat, check our phones, eat, check our phones and sleep. What ever happened to asking your parents “how has your day been?” or “Mum, I’m stressed with school/uni”

P88 if you were to pass away right now, would you be able to look back and say to yourself “i did everything I could”?

P89 if there was one thing i could say to my past self, it would be “enjoy it. Don’t stress. The stuff you’ve got going on right now is nothing. Enjoy your childhood. Cause from where im coming from, i miss it. I should have taken advantage of it”

P90 sometimes i think to myself “everyday, its the same thing. Wake up, uni, computer, eat, uni, computer. Will life continue to be the same, dead, endless cycle? Is that what we’ve been brought up for? To work our asses off in school/uni only to end up in a dead, endless cycle”

P94 Life tires us out sometimes. Perhaps Life’s too difficult
